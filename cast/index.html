Meet the Performers

The cast who makes magic real.

Sophia Carraro is the owner, founder and President of Sophia's Magical Princess Party! Sophia took a short leave from February-August 2019 while she participated in the Disney College Program! Usually you'll see her as most of the characters offered. She attended college for theatre arts and cosmetology and definitely puts her skills to use!

Abigail Heisler is a proud part-time performer for Sophia's Magical Princess Party. Her roles consist of The Beauty Princess and Sleeping Beauty. A University of Houston alumni, she works as an Administrative Assistant for a global eFuels company. She is also a big sister to 5 kids! Abigail anticipates creating magical moments with your children that will make their dreams come true!

Olivia Bell is a part-time performer at Sophia's Magical Princess Party. She has been in the princess industry for over 8 years and is seen mostly as the Snow Princess, the Beauty Princess, and Cinderella. In her free time she enjoys musical theatre and spending time with her dog Bear! Olivia can’t wait to help make your child’s event magical!

Natalia Soriano is a part-time performer for SMPP. You can see her playing The Little Mermaid, The Polynesian Princess and more! Natalia has been involved with theatre for over 10 years and was a camp counselor for the YMCA for one. Natalia couldn’t be more excited to be back with SMPP!

KiLee Thompson is a full-time performer for Sophia's Magical Princess Party. She is a graduate of the University of Houston. She has been involved in theatre for over 8 years and now occasionally helps design costumes for her local theatre!

Keiko Sonoda is a full time performer with SMPP! She is an actor/singer/dancer from the greater Houston area who loves the color green and a good laugh! On hot days she likes a nice fruity drink and when it’s sweater weather she’s a big fan of a hot latte with whipped cream! She has danced on and off since she was very young & involved in musical theatre throughout high school and years after! She can’t wait to bring the joy of these characters to her community!

Gabriella is a full time performer who is mostly seen playing The Miracle Keeper, The Arabian Princess, and The Polynesian Princess! She loves bringing these magical characters to life!

Sofia is a full time performer who can mostly be seen playing The Miracle Keeper, The Polynesian Princess, and more! She is so grateful to be a part of the magic!

Rachel has a background in theater and has performed across multiple states. She is originally from Arizona, where she won Artist of the Year for the National Youth Arts Awards. She recently graduated from UHCL with a B.A. in Literature. When she is not stepping into the glass slippers, she teaches middle school English. She is looking forward to creating special moments for every child!

Felicia is a graduate of the University of Texas at Dallas where she studied Design and Theater arts. Performing brings so much joy to her life and she loves to create a magical experience for you!

Anasofia Locklear is a part-time performer for Sophia's Magical Princess Party. She is a full time college student studying to become a teacher. Anasofia grew up in theatre, performing in musicals and plays. As a child she wanted to become a princess, and now that her childhood dream has come true she is thrilled to bring magic to life and help your child’s dream come true!
