Meet the Performers

The cast who makes magic real.

Sophia Carraro is the owner, founder and President of Sophia's Magical Princess Party! Sophia took a short leave from February-August 2019 while she participated in the Disney College Program! Usually you'll see her as most of the characters offered. She attended college for theatre arts and cosmetology and definitely puts her skills to use!

Abigail Heisler is a proud full-time performer for Sophia's Magical Princess Party. Her roles consist of The Beauty Princess and Sleeping Beauty. A University of Houston alumni, she works as an Administrative Assistant for a global eFuels company. She is also a big sister to 5 kids! Abigail anticipates creating magical moments with your children that will make their dreams come true!

Olivia Bell is a full-time performer at Sophia's Magical Princess Party. She has been in the princess industry for 8 years and is seen mostly as the Snow Princess, the Beauty Princess, and Cinderella. In her free time she enjoys musical theatre and spending time with her dog Bear! Olivia can’t wait to help make your child’s event magical!

Natalia Soriano is a full-time performer for SMPP. You can see her playing The Little Mermaid, The Latina Princess and more! Natalia has been involved with theatre for 10 years and was a camp counselor for the YMCA for one. Natalia couldn’t be more excited to be back with SMPP!

KiLee Thompson is a full-time performer for Sophia's Magical Princess Party. She is a student at the University of Houston studying Communications and Nonprofit. She has been involved in theatre for 8 years and now occasionally helps design costumes for her local theatre!

Savannah Leary is a part-time performer for Sophia's Magical Princess Party! Her roles consist of The Snow Princess and Rapunzel. She is a senior in high school who is part of the PALs program, where she mentors children. In her free time, you can find her dancing at the studio, singing in choir, or playing with her dogs! Savannah cannot wait to make your event magical!

Nayelly Ortiz is a part-time performer at Sophia's Magical Princess Party! She is seen playing as The Polynesian Princess, The Arabian Princess, The Latina Princess and more. She loves theatre, dance and anything that is allowing her to exercise her creative side. She is currently in school studying for a bachelors in business marketing and hopes to someday own a local coffee shop. She started singing and dancing at a young age and is excited to meet your young ones soon!

Taylor Greny is a part-time performer, and is so happy to be a part of Sophia's Magical Princess Party! She's seen as The Scottish Princess, The Little Mermaid and others. She's currently studying a BFA in Acting and spends her afternoons drawing and crocheting. She's so excited to make your childs perty absolutely enchanted.
