Performance FAQs

What is the setlist?

We provide a concert-like setlist that we promise fans will love. If you'd like to review it beforehand, we would be happy to have you adjust it and make requests.

This must be done at least a week before your event. Be aware that some songs may include curse words and adult topics.

How long is the setlist?

We will make the setlist last a majority of the time you book. I.e. if you book two hours instead of one, you will experience a longer performance (who doesn't love more songs?)

If you'd like to shorten the time of the actual performance to make time for other activities, this must be discussed beforehand.

What equipment do you use?

We will provide our own microphone, receiver, small speaker, and music. For bigger events with equipment or DJs, please discuss the set up with us beforehand.

Can Taylor give me an autograph?

Our impersonators will decline any and all autograph requests.

How do costume changes work?

We offer multiple different outfits. If you'd like costume changes at your event, a private/discreet area must be provided close by and discussed beforehand. Some costume changes only have a few seconds to pull off!
